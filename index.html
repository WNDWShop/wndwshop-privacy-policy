Privacy Policy for WNDWShop

Effective Date: 10/1/2024

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use our extension:

  • Personal Information: Your email address, collected for authentication purposes.
  • Avatar Creation Data: Images uploaded for avatar creation.
  • Virtual Try-On Data: Clothing product information from supported e-commerce websites.
  • E-commerce or Online Clothing Store Data: Name, URL, and information regarding e-commerce and online clothing websites.
  • Cookies and Session Data: Cookies to manage session information during authentication.

2. How We Use the Information

Your data is used for the following purposes:

  • Authentication: To securely sign you in.
  • Avatar Creation and Try-On: To create your avatar and facilitate virtual try-on experiences.
  • Enhancing User Experience: To personalize your experience by storing preferences and try-on history.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or trade your personal information with third parties. Data is shared only with AWS for storage and processing purposes.

4. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously, employing encryption and secure storage methods throughout our browser extension.

5. User Control and Data Deletion

You can request to delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at admin@wndwshop.me.

6. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Any changes will be posted on this page, and significant changes will be communicated via email.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, feel free to contact us at admin@wndwshop.me.
